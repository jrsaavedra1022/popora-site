Support

Need help with Popora? We are here to assist with gameplay, settings, bug reports, and general app questions.

Contact

Email us at: popora.support@gmail.com

When contacting support, include your device model, OS version, and a short issue description.

FAQ

How do I disable music or sound effects?

Open the Settings screen in Popora and toggle Music or Sound Effects as needed.

Why are ads shown in the app?

Ads help support development. In Popora, ads are placed in Home and Settings screens, not during active gameplay.

I found a bug. What should I do?

Send a report by email with steps to reproduce, screenshots if available, and your device details.
