1. General Information

This Privacy Policy explains how Jose Saavedra handles information related to the mobile game Popora. The app is available on iPhone and Android.

2. Information We Collect

Popora does not require account registration. Depending on your device and platform, the app and integrated services may process:

  • Advertising identifiers (such as IDFA/AAID) used by advertising partners.
  • Basic app interaction events (for app performance, ad delivery, and analytics, if enabled).
  • Local settings/preferences stored on your device (e.g., sound, music, vibration, dark mode).

3. Advertising and Third Parties

Popora uses third-party advertising services, including Google AdMob, to show ads in non-gameplay screens (such as Home and Settings).

These partners may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies, including advertising identifiers and device information.

Please review Google policies here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. How Data Is Used

  • To improve app stability and user experience.

5. Data Security

We apply reasonable measures to protect data processed by the app. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
